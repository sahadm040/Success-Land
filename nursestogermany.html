Process

Requirements:

Success Land is a strong implementation consulting & support company specialized in supporting your recruitment needs. We look a suitable employer for you.

  • MSc/BSc/GNM
  • Age below 43 years
  • Freshers can also apply

Send your CV

"Since it will be the first thing a recruiter sees when you send a resume through email, the substance of the message is quite crucial. That may determine whether or not your resume gets reviewed. Because you want everything to be perfect—the subject line, the greetings, the email content, the sign-offs, and of course your resume—it is still a good idea to think about what to write in an email when sending a resume."

Send the copies of needed documents

"Three sets of documents make up our whole documentation process - documents for profile assessment, documents for application, and documents for visa application."

Join the language course(If needed)

" Languages are a very interesting choice for a course as you get to learn new languages. The candidate must decide on which language he/she wants to learn (If needed)."

Submit the documents for translation and recognition process.

"If the competent authority requires “original” documents, then you should submit officially certified copies. Do not send originals"

Interview with Employer in Germany(Once you start B1)

" You should be able to explain exactly why this company is interesting to you. If you come from another country, you may be asked why you want to work in Germany and how good your German skills are. End of the interview Give your interviewers positive feedback. They should know that you would like to accept the job."

Offer Letter from hospital

"If the HR hasn’t sent the offer letter, wait for 7–10 days. If you still do not receive the offer, there is nothing you can really do about it, other than following up with the HR on phone or through email."

Visa application

"Assuming you have all documents cleared and an offer letter from employer is signed by you, It would take around 3–4 working days for issuance of Employment visa."

Fly to Germany & Start work

"We wish a safe and joyful journey"
