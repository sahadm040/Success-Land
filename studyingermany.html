Study Nursing In Germany

"Ausbildung is a highly competitive training. It is suitable for all those, who want to learn a profession according to German standards – including the perspective to work and live in Germany even as a non-EU citizen. From the employing hospital students get a monthly salary. The vocational training takes usually between 2 and 4 years. You can select from short apprenticeships and longer apprenticeships in Germany. You will learn your profession to 50% within the company by practical work and to 50% in a special vocational school. Lessons will be held in German only – therefore you need German skills on level B2. During the training in Germany, you will be employed by a German hospital with a German contract providing the same rights and obligations as for German nationals. With Success Land, you will get support to study nursing in Germany after school."
